ImpromptuAI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

ImpromptuAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we store it when you use the ImpromptuAI app.

Data We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Apple, we collect your Apple User ID and, where provided by Apple, your email address (which may be an Apple Private Relay email address) to create and manage your account.
  • User Prompts: When you input a description for music generation, your prompt is securely sent to our servers and AI providers to generate music.
  • Generated Content: Generated music scores and audio files may be temporarily stored to provide the requested service.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic information such as token usage and the number of music generations to operate and improve the app.
  • Performance Data: We collect technical information to monitor reliability, maintain security, diagnose issues and improve the performance of the service.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide music generation and other core app functionality.
  • To manage your account, purchases and token balance.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To monitor performance, maintain security, investigate issues and improve the app.
  • To contact you regarding your account, important service-related information, customer support, or to request feedback about your experience using ImpromptuAI.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your location, contacts or financial information. Purchases are securely processed by Apple.

Data Storage and Retention

Your account information, token balance and purchase history are stored securely on our backend. Prompts, generated scores and audio files may be stored temporarily as required to provide the service, improve reliability and assist with customer support.

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. You may contact us to request deletion of your account and associated personal information, subject to any legal requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
